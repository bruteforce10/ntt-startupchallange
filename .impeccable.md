## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are startups exploring the NTT Startup Challenge website to understand the program, evaluate fit, and take action quickly. Secondary users include potential partners and attendees who need a clear path into registration and collaboration opportunities.

### Brand Personality
Corporate, modern, and credible. The interface should feel established, globally minded, and confident without becoming cold or overly enterprise-heavy.

### Aesthetic Direction
Follow the existing NTT Startup Challenge visual language: bright white surfaces, crisp blue accents, strong information hierarchy, and polished business-event clarity. Motion and interaction should feel restrained, fast, and intentional.

### Design Principles
1. Keep the primary path obvious: registration should be the clearest action.
2. Preserve corporate clarity: use restrained contrast and clean spacing over decorative noise.
3. Support secondary actions without competing with the primary CTA.
4. Adapt interactions by context: desktop can progressively disclose, mobile should keep important actions directly reachable.
5. Match the existing site language before introducing new visual ideas.
